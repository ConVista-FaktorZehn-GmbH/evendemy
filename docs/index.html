Evendemy

What is Evendemy?

Evendemy is a open-source community webapp for your company! Create appointments for your trainings, meetings or for anything worth celebrating. With Evendemy this possibility is just a few clicks far away.

Each meeting you create is visible for all members and everyone can participate! With this tool you can strengthen the sense of community at your company and bring people together. You can offer courses easily and therefore distribute knowledge faster and in a better way.

Evendemy is built to be easy to use. Create meetings and everyone in your community will be informed with a mail about it. People who are interessted can tell you that they want to participate with just one click.
Next, you will be informed and the participant will get a notification via mail with a calendar entry. Everything is transparent. After your meeting, you can decide who really took part. This is a really easy solution that can enhance your "work together".

How can I use it?

It is really easy to use. Just download and install mongodb. Make sure you have installed node.js and angular-cli. And of course you have to clone the evendemy.

Here is the documentation for the Server and the Frontend. Follow these steps and you can start your very own evendemy!

Please note that the version of evendemy on Github is just for demonstration. You have to configure it yourself in the server/config.json and you have to implement your own authentification in the server/auth.js file.

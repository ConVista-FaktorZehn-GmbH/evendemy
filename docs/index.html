Your open-source community webapp!

What is Evendemy?

Evendemy is a open-source community webapp for your company! Create appointments for your trainings, meetings or for anything worth celebrating. With Evendemy this possibility is just a few clicks far away. Evendemy is a plattform where you can manage all these meetings and invite everyone to be part of.

How Evendemy works

Let's say, you want to go to the cinema with colleagues. Evendemy can help you. It is really easy to use Evendemy.

  • First step: create a meeting. Every information is optional - but better information will help you for a more successfull meeting.
  • Evendemy notifies everyone about the new meeting.
  • Interested people can participate. They get a mail with a calendar entry and you will also be notified.
  • Have a great time!
  • After having a wonderful time: you can confirm who participated and the confirmed attendees will see that.

An easy but powerful way to manage and share some wonderful moments.

Advantages and disadvantages compared to simple calendar invitations

You maybe ask yourself what is the benefit compared to simple calendar invitations or other solutions. Here is some help.

Advantages of Evendemy
  • One place where all meetings will be collected
  • Every Meeting has the same structure
  • You see which events are upcoming
  • Everyone is invited to participate to the meetings
  • Everyone can see who want to participate
  • Meetings can be commented - every attendee will be informed
  • You can see each meeting where you took part
Disadvantages of Evendemy
  • Attendees will receive a mail with a calendar entry (even for each relevant change) - but you have to accept or delete it manually by yourself.

Most of the advantages can not be achieved with a simple calendar invitation. That is why Evendemy is such a great solution for the problem.
